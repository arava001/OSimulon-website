About Me

I am passionate about Automated Fiber Placement (AFP) technology and its applications in advanced composites for aerospace and automotive industries. My PhD research focuses on enhancing AFP precision, efficiency, and control by improving toolpath planning and thermal/resin management. I integrate multiphysics modeling — including advanced finite element analysis and resin behavior simulations — with rigorous experimentation to optimize composite quality and performance.
